Jura

Jura is a beautiful remote island in the Inner Hebrides of Scotland where Christy has spent many holidays with family and friends. Jura is known for its three paps (mountains) and having more deer than people! It has a food shop, a couple of small cafes and a distillery. It takes about 7-8 hours to get to Jura from Glasgow, so we recommend that you stay a few days. There is a hotel in Craighouse by the pier where the wedding celebration meal and ceilidh dance will be held.
